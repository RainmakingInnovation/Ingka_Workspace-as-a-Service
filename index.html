Hello! Welcome to our startup compass. This is developed with information from 340+ startups to visualise and show key venture activities of ‘Co-working at INGKA’ and ‘Workspace sourcing – Offices’ plays.

You can move your mouse to hover on all startups to see more information, and you can click on them to go to their websites. There are 2 toggles to visualise some insights. The top toggle rearranges the startups by founded year, funding stage, or maturity; the bottom toggle is used to either show all startups with the same opacity or highlight the 'Hero' startups by making non-'Hero' startups slightly transparent.

Hope you find this compass useful!
